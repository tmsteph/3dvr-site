Homemade AI Chips & Computers

This page explores how ideas from tiny corp and Atomic Semi can inspire a path toward more open, accessible AI hardware and homemade computers.

Vision

Start with software, tooling, boards, and prototypes. Learn the stack step by step. Build toward open systems ordinary people can understand, modify, and eventually manufacture more locally.

Roadmap

Software and compilers → accelerator boards → prototype systems → custom chips → more open personal computers.

Why it matters

Open hardware can make computing more understandable, repairable, and participatory.
